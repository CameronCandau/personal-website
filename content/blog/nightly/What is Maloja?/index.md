---
title: What is Maloja?
date: 2026-07-22
---
https://github.com/krateng/maloja

- A self-hosted music scrobbling server!
- Useful for tracking listening trends without using an external service like [LastFM](https://www.last.fm/) or [Listenbrainz](https://listenbrainz.org/)
- Allows you to own your listening data while scrobbling to additional services as well -- you can still scrobble to LastFM for discovery while keeping personal record/backups
- You don't have to let Spotify lie to you about your listening patterns!
	- https://www.mirror.co.uk/news/weird-news/spotify-wrapped-ruined-after-people-36349234
	- https://community.spotify.com/t5/Your-Library/My-Wrapped-content-is-inaccurate/td-p/7242372)
- ...or trust that an external party will always be able to protect (the confidentiality, integrity, and availability) of your listening data
